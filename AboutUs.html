Our History

Founded in 2010, C'Groovy started as a small startup with a passion for bringing music lovers together. From humble beginnings as a local streaming platform, we have grown into a vibrant global community that connects artists and fans through innovative music services and live events. Our dedication to quality sound, cutting-edge technology, and authentic experiences has driven every step of our journey.

Our Journey

2010 - Inception

C'Groovy was founded in a small garage with a vision to transform the music streaming experience.

2013 - First Millions

We reached 1 million active users and launched our mobile app for broader access.

2017 - Live Events

Expanded to producing live music events, connecting fans with artists face to face.

2022 - Global Expansion

Entered new international markets, growing our community worldwide.
